Withdrawal Request

Cash Out Your Winnings Securely

Ready to enjoy your profits from Golden Ace Poker Club? Our withdrawal process is designed to be efficient and secure. Whether you’re cashing out chips or requesting a crypto transfer, we’ll guide you through the steps.

1. Eligibility & Verification

  • Legal Age & Terms Compliance: You must meet our Terms of Service and legal age requirements.
  • KYC (if needed): We may request identification documents to confirm your identity, especially for larger withdrawals.
  • Minimum Balance: Ensure you have the required minimum balance in your account before requesting a payout.

2. Withdrawal Options

You can withdraw via one of the following methods:

  • Crypto Transfer: BTC, ETH, USDT, or other agreed-upon tokens (please fill out the form below).
  • Chip Conversion: Convert your in-game chips to a designated crypto balance, then proceed with a blockchain transfer.
  • (Future Update) - We may add direct bank transfer or e-wallet solutions based on user demand and regulatory conditions.

3. Request Form

Note: Our team verifies each request to maintain security and compliance with applicable laws. Verification typically takes under 3 hours, but we reserve up to 24 hours if further checks are required.

4. Timeline & Fees

  • Standard Processing: 1–3 hours for most transactions.
  • Possible Delays: Up to 24 hours if manual verification is needed or during high network congestion (e.g., Bitcoin mempool spikes).
  • Network Fees (Crypto): You are responsible for any blockchain transaction fees (miners’ fees). We don’t add hidden fees on top of that.

Got Questions?

If you encounter issues or have additional questions regarding withdrawals, check our FAQ or contact us directly via live chat.

Golden Ace Poker Club strives to ensure smooth payouts so you can enjoy your winnings without hassle!
