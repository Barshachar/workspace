Crypto Payment

Fast, Secure Cryptocurrency Transactions

Golden Ace Poker Club offers the convenience of digital currency for buying chips, paying club fees, or entering special events. We believe in fast, borderless transactions that let you focus on the game rather than payment hassles.

Supported Coins & Networks

  • Bitcoin (BTC): Mainnet (Layer 1). The most recognized crypto—secure, global, and proven.
  • Ethereum (ETH): ERC-20 network. Quick processing, vast ecosystem, ideal for stablecoins.
  • Tether (USDT): Supports ERC-20 or TRC-20 (verify before sending). Great for maintaining a stable USD-equivalent balance.
  • BNB Chain (optional): We may accommodate BEP-20 tokens if arranged in advance. Contact support for details.

Don’t see your preferred coin? Contact us to discuss alternative options.

Payment Instructions

  1. Obtain Our Wallet Address: We’ll provide a unique deposit address (e.g., BTC, ETH). Or scan the QR code if available.
  2. Send the Desired Amount: Double-check the network (ERC-20, TRC-20, etc.) and the exact address. Any mistake could result in loss of funds.
  3. Copy the TxID / Transaction Hash: After sending, get the transaction details from your wallet or exchange.
  4. Fill the Form Below: Let us know which coin, how much, and your ClubGG in-game name so we can credit your account.

Crypto Payment Form

We typically verify payments within 30-60 minutes, but please allow up to 24 hours in rare cases (e.g., network congestion, manual checks).

Security & Guidelines

Always confirm the correct wallet address and network before sending. We are not responsible for funds sent to an incorrect address or on the wrong chain.

Tip: If you’re new to crypto, consider sending a small test transaction first to ensure everything is correct.
