Responsible Gaming

Our Commitment to a Safe & Enjoyable Environment

At Golden Ace Poker Club, we strive to create a positive and engaging poker experience for all players. We believe gaming should be fun and competitive, not a means to cope with financial, psychological, or personal difficulties.

1. Preventing Underage Play

We do not allow players who are below the legal gambling age in their jurisdiction. We may request ID verification to ensure compliance. If you suspect an underage user, please contact us immediately.

2. Setting Personal Limits

Every player has a responsibility to set personal limits—time and money—to keep their poker activity under control.

  • Budget: Decide how much you can comfortably afford to lose before playing. Never chase losses by adding more money spontaneously.
  • Session Time: Take regular breaks. Avoid lengthy sessions that might lead to fatigue or impulsive decisions.
  • Self-Assessment: Periodically ask yourself if gaming is still fun or if it’s becoming compulsive.

3. Recognizing Problem Gambling

Poker should be a form of entertainment, not a livelihood or an escape from real-life problems. You may need help if you:

  • Find it difficult to stop playing even when losing money or time you can’t afford
  • Feel restless or irritable when not playing
  • Neglect other responsibilities or relationships due to poker
  • Try to recoup losses immediately by betting more

If any of these signs apply, we encourage seeking assistance.

4. Self-Exclusion & Support

Golden Ace Poker Club offers self-exclusion measures for those who recognize they need a break:

  • Temporary Exclusion: Suspend access to your account for a set period (days, weeks, or months).
  • Permanent Exclusion: If you require a longer-term or indefinite break, we can close your account permanently upon request.
  • Resources: Organizations like Gamblers Anonymous and other counseling services provide professional help. If you need guidance, feel free to contact us for references.

We will promptly assist in self-exclusion requests and ensure your account remains inaccessible during the specified period.

5. Our Responsibility

We maintain strict policies and monitoring to:

  • Prevent minors from accessing our services
  • Detect and act on suspicious or harmful behavior
  • Collaborate with regulatory bodies and counseling organizations as needed

Together, we can cultivate a balanced, respectful environment where everyone enjoys the competitive nature of poker without undue harm.

Need More Help?

If you or someone you know might have a gambling problem, we encourage seeking professional help. For more information or self-exclusion requests, contact our support.
