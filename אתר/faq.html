Frequently Asked Questions

General Queries

1. Is it safe to play online poker here?

Absolutely. We operate on the reputable ClubGG platform, ensuring secure and fair play. We also have strict rules against cheating or collusion, backed by manual security checks.

2. How do I join the club?

Download the ClubGG app (mobile or emulator on PC), create an account, and enter our Club ID 729608. Alternatively, fill out the Join the Club form on this site for manual approval.

3. What are the legal age requirements?

You must meet the minimum legal gambling age in your jurisdiction (often 18 or 21), as specified in our Terms. We may request proof of age if there’s any doubt.

Payment & Crypto

4. Which cryptocurrencies do you accept?

We currently accept Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT), and sometimes BNB Chain (BEP-20) upon request. Check our Crypto Payment page for details.

5. How do I make a deposit using crypto?

Send funds to our provided wallet address, then fill out the payment info form on Crypto Payment. We verify and credit your account as soon as possible.

6. How long does withdrawal take?

Typical withdrawals process in 1–3 hours, but we reserve up to 24 hours for verification. Check Withdrawal for more info.

Gameplay & Tournaments

7. Do I need a certain bankroll to start?

Not necessarily. We have tables suitable for micro-stakes players and high-stakes professionals. Pick a level that matches your comfort zone.

8. What types of games do you offer?

Primarily Texas Hold’em and Pot-Limit Omaha, plus occasional Mixed Games. See our Club Guide for details.

9. How do tournaments work?

We have scheduled tournaments with varying buy-ins, freerolls for practice, and high-roller events for bigger prize pools. Find the schedule in the ClubGG app or on our News page.

Security & Fair Play

10. What if I suspect cheating or collusion?

Contact us immediately. We have manual checks, and if any player is found violating our Terms of Service, we remove them to maintain game integrity.

11. Are my funds safe?

Yes. We store any club funds securely, often on secure crypto wallets. We also track in-game chips meticulously to ensure accurate balances.

12. Can I report toxic behavior in chat?

Absolutely. We encourage respectful communication. If someone is abusive, inform us so we can issue warnings or bans as needed.

Still Need Answers?

  • Use our live chat widget (bottom-right corner)
  • Check the News page for updates
