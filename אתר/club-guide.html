Club Guide

Welcome to Our Professional Poker Lounge

Golden Ace Poker Club provides a premium poker environment on the ClubGG platform. Below is an overview of the various games and tournaments we offer, along with the community guidelines and professional standards that help us maintain a first-class experience.

Types of Games

  • Texas Hold’em (No-Limit): Our flagship game, ranging from micro-stakes to high-stakes tables. A perfect choice for newcomers and veterans alike.
  • Pot-Limit Omaha (PLO): For those who enjoy more action and bigger pots, Omaha games are available around the clock.
  • Mixed Games (Rotation): Occasionally we host rotation tables (e.g., Hold’em/PLO/Razz) for players seeking variety and a greater challenge.

Tournaments

We host daily and weekly tournaments with buy-ins suitable for all bankrolls. Watch for:

  • Freerolls: Great for practice or building a bankroll from scratch.
  • Regular Scheduled Tournaments: Fixed buy-ins, decent prize pools, and a strong field of competitors.
  • High Roller Events: Exclusive invites or larger buy-ins for seasoned pros seeking substantial rewards.
  • Special Series & Championship Events: Multi-day series with guaranteed prize pools, satellite entries, and recognition for top finishers.

Check the Tournaments tab within the app or our News page for the latest schedule.

Cash Games

Our cash tables run 24/7 with blinds ranging from micro-stakes (NL2) to high-stakes (NL1000+). You’ll find consistent action at any hour:

  • Micro/Low Stakes: Ideal for beginners or those experimenting with new strategies.
  • Mid-Stakes: Balanced competition, moderate variance, and decent winnings potential.
  • High Stakes: Not for the faint of heart—this arena sees big pots and skilled players.

Tables are typically short-handed (6-max) or full-ring (9 players). We also sometimes open Heads-Up (1v1) tables on demand.

Professional Standards & Etiquette

Golden Ace Poker Club fosters a respectful, competitive environment. Please uphold our professional standards:

  • Respect Others: Treat fellow players politely. No harassment, offensive language, or disruptive behavior.
  • Fair Play: Collusion, chip dumping, and multi-accounting are strictly prohibited. Violations result in immediate ban.
  • Game Integrity: We utilize security checks on suspicious play patterns. If you spot unethical behavior, report it to staff.
  • Compliance: You must follow the Terms of Service and ensure you meet the legal age requirements in your jurisdiction.

Need Help or More Information?

If you have questions regarding game formats, tournament schedules, or table availability, feel free to:

  • Use our live chat (bottom-right corner).
  • Read our FAQ or News page for updates.
  • Contact us directly via the Contact page.
