Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Golden Ace Poker Club (“the Club”). We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is protected. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Details: When you sign up or fill out forms (like Join the Club, Contact, or Crypto Payment), we collect personal data such as name, email address, in-game ID, etc.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personal info (like IP address, device type, browser version) to enhance user experience and for security checks.
  • Transaction Data: If you make deposits or withdrawals in cryptocurrency, we may record wallet addresses, transaction IDs, and related financial data (excluding private keys).

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information collected to:

  • Process membership applications and manage your account
  • Verify identity or age (KYC) where required
  • Facilitate crypto payments and track balances
  • Provide customer support or respond to inquiries
  • Improve site functionality and user experience

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to remember user preferences and gather analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser, but this may affect certain features.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • Third-Party Services: We may share data with trusted providers (e.g., email services, payment processors) strictly for operational purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose info if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or user safety.
  • No Selling of Data: We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or as required by law. For instance, if we must keep records for KYC or regulatory compliance, we will store them securely.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us.

9. Underage Players

Golden Ace Poker Club does not knowingly collect information from users under the legal gambling age. If you suspect someone underage has provided personal data, let us know so we can remove it.

10. International Transfers

If you access the Club from outside the jurisdiction of our primary servers, your data may be transferred across borders, subject to local data protection laws.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will be posted here, and by continuing to use our services, you accept any changes.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Policy, please visit our Contact page or reach us via live chat. We aim to reply promptly.
