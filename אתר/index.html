Welcome to Golden Ace Poker Club

Experience the thrill of online poker in a world-class environment. At Golden Ace Poker Club, we provide daily tournaments, private tables, competitive gameplay, and a secure crypto payment system—all on the robust ClubGG platform.

Why Choose Golden Ace?

  • Exclusive Online Poker Community: We handpick our members to maintain a respectful, fair-play atmosphere with real contenders at every table.
  • Daily Tournaments & Events: Whether you’re a beginner or a pro, our wide range of buy-ins and formats keeps the action fresh and exciting every single day.
  • Secure Crypto Payments: Enjoy seamless deposits and withdrawals using Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other popular cryptocurrencies—fast, global, and trusted.
  • Advanced Platform: Built on ClubGG, our club ensures stability, anti-cheating measures, and a polished user interface for both mobile and desktop.
  • 24/7 Support: Our dedicated team is here to assist you around the clock via live chat or email. We value quick responses to keep you focused on the game.

Supported Currencies & Networks

We currently support the following cryptocurrencies and networks:

  • Bitcoin (BTC) – Mainnet
  • Ethereum (ETH) – ERC-20 on Ethereum mainnet
  • Tether (USDT) – ERC-20 or TRC-20 (verify beforehand)
  • BNB Chain (optional) – BEP-20 tokens upon request

For exact details, visit Crypto Payment or Contact Us.

Community & Fair Play

We believe in building a vibrant community of passionate poker players.
Fairness is central to our operation: no cheating, chip dumping, or collusion is tolerated. Our security measures and manual checks ensure a balanced field.
